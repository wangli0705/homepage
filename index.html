Introduction

Welcome to my academic homepage! Here, you can find information about my research interests, publications, and contributions to conferences. My work primarily focuses on the application of artificial intelligence in education, with a special interest in language learning technologies.

Research Interests

My research interests include artificial intelligence in education, computational linguistics, and the development of interactive tools for language learning. I am particularly fascinated by the potential of AI to personalize learning experiences and improve educational outcomes.
