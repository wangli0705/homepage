Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Education, Zhejiang University, located in Hangzhou, China. My research primarily focuses on education technology, Chinese language education, and feedback mechanisms. I am also interested in the application of large language models in educational settings, particularly in facilitating Chinese language learning for students in Singapore.

Till now, I have authored numerous papers, with several published in prestigious journals and conferences. My work has been recognized with various awards and honors, reflecting the impact and significance of my research in the field.

I am on track to receive my PhD degree in June 2024. Currently, I'm actively seeking opportunities as a research scientist or postdoc. Please don't hesitate to reach out!
